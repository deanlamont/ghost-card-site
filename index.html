How It Works

Four steps. The guy explaining his disc flight path on hole 3 can manage it.

No tutorial. No onboarding. No seven-screen walkthrough explaining what a birdie is. You know what a birdie is. The ghosts know what a birdie is. Let's go.

Step 01

Pick your course

7,008 US disc golf courses. Search by name or zip. Par data is verified where possible — and community-corrected everywhere else, because someone always knows a course better than the internet does.

Step 02

Build your card

Add real players, invite friends, or drop in ghost pros. Gannon Buhr rates a 1027. He will birdie holes you didn't think were birdie-able. He will not apologize. He is a ghost. He has no concept of feelings.

Step 03

Score hole by hole

One-handed input. Live leaderboard rearranges itself after every hole like a horror film cast list — people you thought were safe suddenly aren't. The feeling of watching your lead evaporate on hole 14 is character-building, probably.

Step 04

Haunt your history

Every round saved. Every hole documented. The app keeps a perfect record of every decision you've ever made on a disc golf course, which is either motivating or deeply haunting depending on how hole 7 went.
