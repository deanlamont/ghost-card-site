Multiplayer Horror Mode

A card game. Inside your round. Against your friends.

50 cards. 4 types. One card per hole. You lose a hole, you draw. You play a card, someone throws non-dominant hand on the next tee. The disc golf still counts.

Played anytime. Mirror, cancel, copy, or amplify. The chaos variable.
