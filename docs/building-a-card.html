Building a Card

Courses, players, ghost opponents, formats. Everything on the scorecard before the disc leaves your hand.

Docs / Building a Card

Course search

Ghost Card includes 7,008 US disc golf courses from the PDGA course directory. Search by name or city. Each course has at least one layout with hole count and total par.

If par data is wrong, you can fix it — see Community Par Corrections.

Ghost opponents

134 real PDGA-rated pros across MPO and FPO. Ghost scores are simulated using each pro's rating against the course SSA (Scratch Scoring Average).

Top MPO ghosts: Ricky Wysocki (1044), Paul McBeth (1041), Calvin Heimburg (1036), Eagle McMahon (1033), Gannon Buhr (1027). FPO is led by Missy Gannon (1000) and Catrina Allen (988).

Ghost pros throw every hole. Their scores appear on the live leaderboard as cream-colored rows. They never slow down. They never explain their disc choice.

Field presets

Instead of selecting individual ghosts, Ghost Card uses field presets — curated groups of ghost pros at different difficulty levels. Choose a preset and a full field loads automatically.

Field difficulty

Difficulty scales the ghost simulations. Higher difficulty means ghosts play closer to peak ratings. Lower difficulty adds variance — bad rounds happen, just like real tournaments.

Division

MPOFPO

Choose MPO (Mixed Pro Open) or FPO (Female Pro Open). This determines which ghost pros populate your leaderboard.

Adding real players

Add up to 3 other players to the card by name. The host keeps the phone — all scores are entered on the same device. Real players and ghost pros appear side by side on the leaderboard.

Friends on your list appear as quick-add chips during setup — one tap to add them.

One device, one card. Ghost Card does not sync scores between phones. The host scores the card for the whole group. There is no live view for other players — that feature is coming in a future update.

Format

Stroke Play — each player scores individually on the host's phone. Lowest total wins.

Best Disc / Scramble — team plays best shot. Host keeps one score per hole. Scramble requires 2+ players.

Personal best ghost

Your personal best ghost (gold row) appears automatically on the leaderboard whenever you've played the course before. Real hole-by-hole scores from your best prior round. Beat it and Round Complete celebrates with a full comparison — holes won, holes lost, strokes gained. No toggle. No setup. That ghost is always there, waiting.

Custom cards

Course not in the database? Tap Create Custom Card. Name the card, set hole count, assign par for each hole manually. Custom cards support all the same features — ghost pros, leaderboard, bag tags.
