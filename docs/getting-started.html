Getting Started

Create an account, sign in, and throw your first round. The ghosts are already on the tee.

Docs / Getting Started

Create an account

Open app.ghostcard.live in any browser. No download required.

Tap Create Account. Enter your email, a password (6+ characters), and a username. Your username becomes your display name on leaderboards and in friend searches.

No account? No problem. Ghost Card works without signing in — you can score rounds and see ghost pros. Rounds just won't be saved, and you won't earn bag tags or track history.

Sign in

Three ways in: Google (one tap), email magic link (no password — enter your email, click the link we send), or email + password. Authentication is handled by Supabase. Your data stays yours.

Installing to your home screen? Open https://app.ghostcard.live in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android) and tap Add to Home Screen from there. Installing from the marketing site pins the wrong thing.

Your first round

After signing in you land on the Play screen. Here's the fastest path to your first round:

  1. Search for a course. Type the name or city. Ghost Card has 7,008 US disc golf courses from the PDGA course directory.
  2. Pick a layout. Most courses have a primary layout pre-selected. Alternate layouts show hole count and total par.
  3. Choose your division. MPO or FPO. This determines which ghost pros appear on your leaderboard.
  4. Tap Play. A full field of rated PDGA ghost pros loads onto the card. You're on the tee.

Score each hole as you play. The live leaderboard updates after every entry. When you finish, tap Complete Round to see your final results, GC Rating, and any bag tags earned.

What happens next

Your round is saved to Round History. Your GC Rating starts tracking. If you submitted any par corrections, those count toward the Bone Collector tag. The ghosts already know your name.
