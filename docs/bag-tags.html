Overview

Ghost Card has 30 collectible bag tags, each referencing a horror film archetype, trope, or specific cinematic moment. Tags are earned exclusively through gameplay — unlocked by specific in-round conditions. They cannot be purchased, gifted, or granted through any mechanism other than doing the thing that unlocks them.

The tone is A24, not Spirit Halloween. The tags reference real cinema. They have custom SVG illustrations. They take the genre seriously, which is exactly why they're worth earning.

Account required

Bag tags require a signed-in account to unlock and store. Tags earned during a guest session are lost when the session ends. Create an account before you try for The Wraith — it only appears once.

Rarity tiers

All 30 tags fall into one of four rarity tiers:

Common
12
Achievable by most players within a handful of rounds
Uncommon
9
Requires consistent play or specific conditions to appear
Rare
6
Hard to achieve, linked to strong performance or unusual events
Legendary
3
Demanding unlock conditions. People whisper about these.

Rarity reflects difficulty to unlock, not arbitrary scarcity. There's no random drop rate. Every tag has a specific, deterministic condition — you either met it or you didn't.

How tags are earned

All tags unlock automatically during or after a round when their conditions are met. You don't need to track or claim them — the app recognizes the condition and adds the tag to your collection. You'll see a notification when a tag unlocks.

Unlock conditions include things like: shooting a specific score on a specific hole number, achieving a run of birdies, completing a round under certain circumstances, playing at unusual times of day, finishing above or below a ghost opponent, and community contributions like par corrections.

The conditions are not published in-app. This is intentional. Figuring out what unlocks a tag is part of the experience. The ghosts know. They just won't tell you directly.

Notable tags

The Haunting Legendary
The app's most demanding tag. Unlock conditions are intentionally obscure — completing specific in-round events under specific circumstances. The Haunting doesn't care about your feelings. It cares about what you did on hole 11.
Bone Collector Legendary
Submit 5 community par corrections. The Bone Collector is watching your corrections. It knows which holes you flagged. Five corrections makes you a legend. See Par Corrections.
The Wraith Legendary
Appears only once — under conditions that may not repeat. If you see the unlock notification for The Wraith and don't have an account, the tag is gone. Sign in before you try for it. You've been warned.
The Entity Rare
Unlocked by outscoring a specific ghost opponent on their best hole type. Strong performance required.
Final Girl Rare
Complete a round in which every other player on the card — real and ghost — finishes over par while you finish under. Survive when no one else does.
Jumpscared Uncommon
Score three consecutive birdies immediately after a double bogey or worse. The comeback after the collapse.
The Séance Uncommon
Complete a round with exactly four ghost opponents on the card — no real players other than yourself. You called them. They answered.
First Blood Common
Save your first round to history. The archive begins.
The Fog Common
Play a round starting before 7am. The woods are watching either way. At 6am, they're just watching harder.

The remaining 21 tags follow similar patterns — specific conditions tied to performance, timing, round composition, or community activity. Explore the Bag Tags tab in-app to see which ones you've unlocked and which remain locked.

Bone Collector in detail

The Bone Collector is one of the three legendary tags and the only one tied entirely to community contribution rather than in-round performance. Submit 5 valid par corrections to Supabase — across any courses, at any point — and the tag unlocks automatically.

The app tracks your correction count. It doesn't reset. Corrections from any round count, not just recent ones. If you've already submitted 3 and submit 2 more, the tag triggers on the 5th.

See the full process in Community Par Corrections.

Equipping a tag

Once unlocked, tags appear in your collection in the Bag Tags tab. Tap any unlocked tag to set it as your active (equipped) tag. Your active tag displays on your profile and appears as a badge on your round history entries.

You can only equip one tag at a time. Switching your equipped tag is free and unlimited — swap it whenever you want. Equipping is display only; it doesn't change unlock conditions or grant any other advantage. It just tells everyone what you've done.
