How It Works

Four steps.
Even the guy with the Bluetooth speaker can manage it.

Ghost Card is a disc golf scoring app. Pick a course, add players, score live, haunt your history. Here's exactly how.

  1. 01

    Pick your disc golf course

    Ghost Card has 7,008 US disc golf courses indexed and searchable by name, city, or zip code. Like the opening scene of a horror film — you choose the location, and then you live with that decision for 18 holes.

    Par data is community-verified where we have it. Spot a wrong par? Submit a correction. Five corrections unlocks the Bone Collector bag tag. Hole 4 is a par 4. The sign has always been wrong.

    7,008 Courses Par correction docs
  2. 02

    Build your scorecard

    Add real players, invite friends to join, or drop in ghost pros from the professional field — real PDGA ratings, real names. Adam Hammes (rated 1029) is on the list. He will not speak. He will just score clean 47s until you respect him.

    Stroke Play, Scramble, or Solo. MPO or FPO. In Scramble mode, enter a team name — it goes on the card. Your dad and a ghost Simon Lizotte is a reasonable group. Your dad and ghost Ricky Wysocki (1044) is a learning experience. Want a multi-round event? Tap Custom Tournament under the Format section.

    Stroke Play · Scramble · Solo · MPO · FPO Card builder docs
  3. 03

    Score your round

    One-hand input designed for the field — the other hand is holding a disc, a coffee, or whatever dignity you have left after hole 6. Running +/− from par updates live. The leaderboard reshuffles after every hole.

    Watch your lead disappear in real time. Very motivating, clinically speaking.

    Live Leaderboard Scoring docs
  4. 04

    Haunt your round history

    Every round saved permanently. Full hole-by-hole breakdown. Every score, every bad lie, every "the wind got it" — all on record. The disc golf scoring app does not forget. The ghosts do not forget.

    Share your round badge. Review any previous card. In horror films, the evidence is always there if you look.

    Full Round Archive History docs

Ready to play?

Ghost Card is free. No downloads. The ghost pros are already on the tee. The group of nine blocking hole 3 will be there regardless.
