Scramble

Everyone throws, group plays from the best disc. One team score per hole. Enter a team name — it goes on the scorecard, the leaderboard, and the results card with your player names listed underneath. Ghost opponents scramble too, sorted into teams. Good for when your dad is on the card and the horror movie has a "we survive together" moment.

Game Format Format guide

Custom Tournament

Name your tournament. Pick 1–5 rounds, Stroke Play or Scramble, up to 4 players. Choose a different course before each round starts — two rounds at Cedar Beach, two at Mossy Bluff, whatever you want. Ghost pro teams get horror-themed names that randomise per tournament. Cumulative leaderboard updates after every round. The Uekman Classic is a real thing now.

Tour Mode

Multi-round tournaments with DGPT-calibrated points. Regular events, Elite+, Majors, and a Finale tier. Create a season, run events across any courses, watch standings update after every round. The ghosts are seeded. They are not nervous.

Horror Mode

A 50-card card game overlay you can add to any multiplayer round. Four card types — Haunt your opponents, play Spirit cards on yourself, hold Reckoning cards until the final hole, and burn Wild cards when things get desperate. Skins run alongside. The card game ends when the round does. Whatever happened on hole 9 stays on hole 9.
